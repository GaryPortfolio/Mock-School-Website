FAQs

Why does this type of academy exist?

Sometimes for kids to learn, they need motivation. That motivation can sometimes come from their hobbies, such as vtubers or anime characters. We built this academy for kids to learn through their hobbies. If they are into anime characters, then having anime characters teach might help them learn more easily.
